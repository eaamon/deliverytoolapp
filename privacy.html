Privacy & Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how Delivery Tool collects, uses, and shares data.

Delivery Tool is committed to protecting your privacy. By using our Google Sheets add-on, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy & Policy.

1. Data Access by the Add-on

The following Google Apps Script permissions (scopes) are requested when you install and use the Delivery Tool add-on:

  • See, edit, create, and delete all your Google Sheets spreadsheets: Needed to read and update your sheets using SpreadsheetApp.
  • Connect to an external service: Allows the add-on to communicate with delivery provider APIs using UrlFetchApp.
  • Allow this application to run when you are not present: Required for time-based triggers or background execution.
  • Display and run third-party web content in prompts and sidebars: Required for custom HTML sidebars or prompts in Google Sheets.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is not stored on any servers. All information stays only within your Google Sheets. Delivery Tool reads and sends data to your selected delivery providers solely to process and track your orders. No personal information is retained, sold, or used for advertising.

3. Data Sharing and External Connections

Delivery Tool only shares Google Sheets data that you explicitly submit through the add-on. Shared data may include order details, recipient names, and addresses. This data is sent only to the delivery providers you select within the add-on (e.g., FedEx, UPS, DHL) for the purpose of order fulfillment. Delivery Tool does not sell or share your data with any other parties.

Some optional features may use third-party services to enhance functionality. In these cases, only the specific data you provide for that feature is shared. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. Please review their privacy policies before using them.

4. Security

All communication with external services is secured via HTTPS. Your spreadsheets remain private, and all data transmissions are encrypted.

5. Your Rights

  • You may uninstall the add-on at any time.
  • You can revoke any permissions through Google Account settings.
  • Deleting your spreadsheet removes all related information.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy & Policy periodically. Continued use of the add-on constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
